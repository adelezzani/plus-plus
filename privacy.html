Privacy Policy

PLUS+ is an IPTV player only. It does not host, stream, upload, or distribute any media content. This page explains the limited data that may be handled by the website and the app.

Information We Collect

The website itself is a static public page. It may collect only standard server and browser information such as basic access logs, device details, and cookies required for site functionality.

App Data

  • PLUS+ does not store or provide IPTV channels, playlists, or media files.
  • If the player saves user preferences locally, they stay on your device unless you delete them.
  • The app should only process playlist or stream links that you provide and are legally allowed to use.

Third-Party Services

If PLUS+ uses third-party services such as TMDB for metadata, those services may receive the information required to respond to your requests. Their own privacy policies apply to that data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We do not intentionally share user information except when required to operate the site, comply with the law, or work with services you directly choose to use.

Children

PLUS+ is not intended for children to use without supervision.

Changes

We may update this policy if the site or player behavior changes.
